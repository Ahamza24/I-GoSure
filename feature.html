Coverage Overview At our core, we are dedicated to delivering optimal insurance value to our clients. Our commitment revolves around providing friendly and professional service, with a primary focus on our customers.

Short-Term Learner Driver Insurance

About the Driver

    Eligibility:
  • Private car drivers aged 18 to 75
  • Commercial vehicle drivers aged 21 to 75
  • Minimum 6 months holding of a Full UK or EU/EEA driver's license (reduced to 3 months for those over 25)
  • Maximum of 2 accidents, claims, or losses in the last 3 years, regardless of fault
  • Maximum of 6 penalty points in the last 3 years
  • UK residency for a minimum of 12 months
  • No insurance refusal, policy cancellation, or voidance history
  • No driving ban in the last 60 months
  • No criminal convictions or pending prosecutions

Impounded Car Insurance

    Criteria:
  • Cars and commercial vehicles (up to 3.5 tonnes)
  • Minimum value for cars and vans: £500
  • Maximum value for cars: £50,000; for vans: £25,000
  • Usage limited to social, domestic, and pleasure, or social, domestic, and pleasure + commercial
  • UK registration, no foreign use or exporting
  • Not left-hand drive, not modified (exceptions for factory-fitted extras, disabled modifications, or LPG conversions)
  • No more than 8 seats, not a rental or hire vehicle (commercial vehicles limited to 5 seats)
  • Valid MOT, not declared as SORN, and not seized by the police

Most asked questions?

This is what we can offer to you!

How to make a claim?

In the event of an incident, we're here to assist you promptly. Please be advised that all claims must be reported within 24 hours of the occurrence. To streamline this process, you can find our contact information in your policy documents. Alternatively, use our convenient in-house claim reporting form available on our website.

What if I haven’t received my email with the documents?

Providing you entered your email address correctly; you will receive an email from us. If you do not receive a confirmation e-mail from us within the hour, please check your spam or junk mail folders. If you have still not received your documents, get in contact with us to request they are sent again.

How long before my vehicle appear on the MID listing?

Once you have purchased a policy from us, your insurance details will be passed to the Motor Insurance Database (MID). They are passed within the timescales required by the MID, but due to the short term nature of these types of insurance, it is possible your policy may have expired before the details are loaded to the database. We always recommend that you print your Insurance Certificate and have this with you as this remains valid proof of your insurance and legal entitlement to drive the vehicle.

How to Cancel Polices?

Our refund policy is determined by the product purchased.

Short Term Car/Van Insurance:
You are entitled to cancel your policy at any time. Due to the short period nature of this policy there will be no refund of premium.
Due to the short term nature of 1 to 28-day policies, there is no cooling-off period.

Monthly Insurance:
There is administrative charge (Please refer to our T&C’s for a breakdown)

Do you insure SORN vehicle?

As long as your vehicle is in a roadworthy condition, and you either have tax or will tax the vehicle with our insurance certificate, we can allow insurance for this purpose. The vehicle must have a current MOT or you must have a pre-booked MOT appointment to attend whilst insured with us in order for the insurance to be valid (please check our insurers criteria before purchasing).
